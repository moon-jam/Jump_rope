Features and Benefits

Jump Rope League offers a range of features to help you make the most of your jump rope experience:

  • Compete with fellow jump ropers from around the world in a friendly and engaging environment.
  • Receive instant feedback and scores for your jump rope videos.
  • Discover tips and techniques from other participants to improve your skills.
  • Stay motivated with our ranking system that rewards your progress and effort.
  • Connect with a community of jump rope enthusiasts and share your achievements.

How to Get Started

Ready to join Jump Rope league? Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Sign in with your Google account by clicking the "Sign in with Google" button above.
  2. Once signed in, you can upload your jump rope videos to participate in the league.
  3. After uploading, our system will analyze your video and provide you with a score and ranking.
  4. Explore the ranking list to see where you stand among other jump rope enthusiasts.
