Upload Video Format:

Accepted formats: '.mp4', '.avi', '.mov', '.mkv', '.webm', '.wmv', '.m4v'

Video length should be between 30 seconds and 1 minute.

Please ensure that the uploaded video is a vertical 16:9 aspect ratio, and make sure that your body and the jump rope are within the frame to avoid any issues during recognition.

Scoring Rules:

open 2 points, cross 3 points, magic cross 5 points.

The final score will be calculated as the rounded result of

((2 * open_count + 3 * cross_count + 5 * magic_cross_count) / video_length) * 30

Example: If a person performs 30 open jumps, 20 cross jumps, and 10 magic cross jumps within 40 seconds, the final score will be
